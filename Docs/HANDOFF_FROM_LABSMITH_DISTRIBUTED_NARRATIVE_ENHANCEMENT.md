## 1. Why this enhancement — SCOPE CLARIFICATION

MicroBeLab is the ONLY sub-cluster app whose CORE DOMAIN (microbiology + immune-response + microbiome simulation) is NOT SEL. The S1 enhancement scope is INTENTIONALLY NARROW: cluster-shared FC + META characters appear ONLY in scenarios touching **microaggression-resilience / community-immune metaphors** (kits 7-9 in MicroBeLab's expansion). The microbiology curriculum (kits 1-6) stays cast-pure (Lacto / Yeast / Photo / Net / Spore / Guard).

The metaphorical bridge: the immune system as a **community of guards** (FC1 Notice + FC7 Consider + FC9 Reflect work alongside Guard, the immune-cell archetype, to model HOW the community holds patterns of harm + repair). This metaphor lets MicroBeLab teach microaggression-resilience WITHOUT requiring the kid to engage real-world identity content (which is InclusionForge's domain).

Per cluster plan § 1.5 + § 1.6, MicroBeLab deploys **3 of 9 FC characters (FC1 Notice / FC7 Consider / FC9 Reflect) + 2 META (META Witness / META Circle) — kits 7+ only**.

## 6. Trauma-informed integrity gates (REQUIRED — beyond standard)

**All 7 Wave 32b gates PRESERVED** (COVID-trauma-sensitive / kits 1-4 beneficial-microbes-first / kit 5 pathogens-opt-in / no villain framing / no COVID-specific scenarios / no mask-or-vax-mandate / no graphic illness). **S1 ENHANCEMENT adds 4 net-new gates**:

### 6.1 Cast-pure kits 1-6 enforcement (CRITICAL — NEW)

Microbiology curriculum kits 1-6 must stay cast-pure. The S1 enhancement introduces cluster-shared cast in kits 7+ ONLY. Mixing microbiology + microaggression-resilience cast in kits 1-6 would dilute both pedagogies.

**Mitigation**:
- `KitMetadata.appearsInKits` for cluster-shared chars enforced via test
- Phase A audit explicitly checks
- Reviewer signoff explicitly tests kits 1-6 cast-purity

### 6.2 Microaggression-attribution non-flattening gate (CRITICAL — NEW)

FC7 Consider's "Maybe many reasons. Let me hold them all." catchphrase IS the canonical microaggression-attribution scaffold per Sue et al. — but it must NOT read as "let me explain away the harm." Holding multiple reasons is NOT the same as dismissing the harm.

**Mitigation**:
- FC7 Consider's intro copy in MicroBeLab kit 8 MUST frame: "Holding many reasons isn't deciding it didn't hurt. The harm is real. The reasons help me decide what to do next."
- Static-response gating for harm-dismissal markers ("maybe they didn't mean it so it's fine" / "I'm probably overreacting") — AI gated to validate-then-redirect
- Reviewer signoff explicitly tests FC7 Consider for harm-non-flattening

### 6.3 META Circle community-of-witness adaptation (NEW)

META Circle in MicroBeLab context becomes both friend-circle AND community-immune-system metaphor. Risk: kid confuses the two; the immune-system-as-community metaphor becomes prescriptive ("your immune system tells you who to trust").

**Mitigation**:
- META Circle intro copy in kit 10 MUST frame the two senses explicitly: "Your circle has two meanings here. (1) The friends and trusted people who see you clearly. (2) The community-of-cells inside you that protects you. Both are real. Both work together."
- Reviewer signoff explicitly tests META Circle's dual-meaning framing

### 6.4 Microbiology + microaggression-resilience cluster-integration gate (NEW)

Kits 13-16 integrate microbiology + microaggression-resilience. The integration must NOT collapse the two domains into "microbiome = microaggression metaphor" (which would be reductive). The metaphor is BRIDGE not COLLAPSE.

**Mitigation**:
- Kits 13-16 framing copy explicitly preserves the two-domain register ("microbiology IS one curriculum; microaggression-resilience IS another; the community-immune-system metaphor BRIDGES them, doesn't merge them")
- Reviewer signoff tests integrated-kit framing
